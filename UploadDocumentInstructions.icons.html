Why You Need to Respond to This Request

Toggle Menu Item Language

You are receiving this request to upload documents because we were unable to verify your identity based on the information you provided on your Unemployment Insurance (UI) claim.

If you did not file this UI claim, select Next and indicate that on the following screen. We investigate all fraud reports.

If you are seeing this request for a second time, you did not respond to the previous request or did not provide sufficient documents to verify your identity. Please provide the required documents.

Avoid Common Mistakes

One of the most common reasons why we can’t verify your identity is because your name, date of birth, or Social Security number do not match the records on file with the Social Security Administration (SSA) or the Department of Motor Vehicles (DMV). For example, you may have changed your name but didn’t notify the DMV.

If you have a name or date of birth discrepancy on your claim, provide documents with your correct name or date of birth, such as an official birth certificate or marriage certificate.

Note: We will not update your information with the SSA, the DMV, or your employer. Contact them directly to make changes.

Submission Deadline

You have 10 calendar days from the Mail Date on your Request for Identity Verification (DE 1326C) mailed to you to respond with proof of identity. However, we encourage you to upload documents as soon as possible to avoid delays in receiving benefits.

Do You Need More Time?

To request additional time, you must call 1-866-401-2849 or mail a request to the address at the top of the Request for Identity Verification (DE 1326C) that was mailed to you. We must receive your request within 10 calendar days of the Mail Date on the paper form.

Penalties

If we do not receive the required documents or a request for more time, or we are not able to verify your identity:

  • You will not be eligible to receive benefits.
  • You will receive a disqualification notice (Notice of Determination) in the mail.
  • You will have to file an appeal to receive benefits for this claim.

Gather Your Documents

For us to verify your identity, you must upload two files:

  • One Photo Identification
  • One Other Identity Document
  • Optional: You can upload up to three additional Identity Documents

Photo Identification

Your photo identification must show your name, date of birth, and photograph. You can use any document from the following list, as long as it has not expired.

  • State identification (ID) card
  • Driver license
  • US passport or passport card
  • US military card (front and back)
  • Military dependent’s ID card (front and back)
  • Permanent Resident Card
  • Certificate of Citizenship
  • Certificate of Naturalization
  • Employment Authorization Document
  • Foreign passport

Other Identity Document

You can use any document from the following list.

  • Employment record
    • W-2 from last year
    • A pay stub or payment statement that shows:
      • An issue date within 12 months prior to the date your claim was filed
      • Your first name (or initial) and last name
      • At least the last four digits of your Social Security number or your employee identification number
      • Your employer’s name
      • The pay period dates or the date it was issued
  • Address verification
    • Any bill or financial statement showing your name and residence address
      • Utility bill (electricity, gas, garbage, water, or sewer)
      • Cable TV or internet bill
      • Telephone bill
      • Bank statement
      • Property tax bill
      • Mortgage statement
    • Lease or rental agreement showing your name and residence address
    • Registration for a PO Box or private mailbox
  • Social Security number
    • Important: A Social Security card will not satisfy this requirement.
    • Social Security statement (all pages)
    • Notice to Third Party of Social Security Number Assignments (SSA-7028)
    • Report of Confidential Social Security Benefit Information (SSA-2458)
  • Date of birth
    • Official birth certificate (with official translation, if any)

Prepare Your Documents for Uploading

The documents you upload must be clear and readable so we can verify your identity. You can either scan them or take a picture with your digital camera or mobile device.

Maximum file size is 25 MB.

Acceptable formats: JPEG, TIFF, PNG, GIF, and PDF.

Your digital documents must be accessible to the device you are going to use to upload them.

Upload Your Documents

After you have prepared your files, select Next and follow the instructions to upload them.

Important: You will have 30 minutes to upload your documents.
